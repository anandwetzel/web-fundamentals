Omelet Recipe

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Toast
  • Butter

Recipe

  1. Add toast to toaster.
  2. Spray a pan with cooking spray.
  3. Crack 2 eggs over pan.
  4. Add salt and pepper.
  5. When eggs are mostly cooked, flip.
  6. Put the eggs on a plate when finished.
  7. Apply butter to toast.
