Penne Vodka Recipe

Ingredients

  • Penne Pasta
  • Heavy Cream
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Olive Oil
  • Butter
  • Vodka
  • Tomato Puree
  • Salt
  • Red Pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta al dente.
  2. Saute the garlic and onions in 2 tablespoons butter and the olive oil
  3. Pour in the vodka carefully
  4. Cook and reduce for 2 to 3 minutes
  5. Pour in the tomato puree and stir
  6. When thoroughly combined reduce the heat to low and pour in the heavy cream.
  7. Stir to combine, then turn heat to the lowest simmer possible. Sprinkle in the red pepper flakes and add salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Finally, stir in 1 tablespoon butter. Pour the drained pasta into the sauce and toss to combine. Sprinkle on the Parmesan, then sprinkle on more red pepper flakes if desired.
